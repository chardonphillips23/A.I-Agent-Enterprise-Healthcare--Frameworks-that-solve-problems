# Business Brief

Creator:
FuturIQ

Audience:
Coaches, consultants, creators, service providers, and entrepreneurs who know AI is important but are overwhelmed by tools and do not know what business system to build first.

Core promise:
Help entrepreneurs build AI-powered business systems that create leverage, leads, and revenue.

Video goal:
Create a sub-15-minute YouTube video that feels practical, advanced, and immediately useful.

Video concept:
Build a Claude Code agent team that turns one business idea into positioning, an offer, YouTube angles, a lead magnet, and a follow-up sequence.

Offer path:
The video should naturally lead to a downloadable Revenue Agent template, a workshop, or a paid AI systems offer.

Voice:
Direct, strategic, high-energy, premium, practical, no fluff.

Constraints:
- Do not make this a generic AI tools tutorial.
- Do not make this a basic prompt engineering lesson.
- Show the connection between AI workflow and revenue.
- Explain that the money is not in tools. The money is in systems.
- Make the system beginner-friendly without making it basic.
