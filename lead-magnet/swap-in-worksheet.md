# Swap-In Worksheet

Fill in the blanks below, then copy each finished paragraph as your prompt to the matching agent. This is the same process used to run "giant teddy bears" and "mobile pet-grooming van" through the pipeline — just with your business instead.

## First: write your business-brief.md

Before running any agent, fill this out and save it as `business-brief.md` in your project root. Every agent reads it first.

```
Creator: [your name or brand]
Audience: [who you're trying to reach — be specific about their situation, not just a job title]
Core promise: [the one outcome you help people get]
Goal: [what you're building this content/system for — a video, a launch, a store, etc.]
Voice: [3-5 words describing your tone]
Constraints: [anything this should NOT sound like, or NOT do]
```

## Step 1: Prompt for market-signal-researcher

> "Business idea to evaluate: [describe your business idea in one or two sentences]. Do your full market-signal-researcher process on this idea: identify the market category, demand signals, audience psychology, sophistication level, and score the opportunity using your scorecard. Return your output in your standard Market Signal Brief format."

## Step 2: Prompt for offer-architect

> "Read business-brief.md first. Here is the market-signal-researcher's output to build from: [paste the full output from Step 1]. Do your full offer-architect process: positioning, core offer, unique mechanism, value ladder, proof requirements, and objection map."

## Step 3: Prompt for content-angle-strategist

> "Read business-brief.md first. Here is the offer-architect's output to build from: [paste the full output from Step 2]. Do your full content-angle-strategist process: best title + why it wins, 5 backup titles, thumbnail text, the first-20-seconds hook word for word, a retention map, and screen recording beats."

## Step 4: Prompt for conversion-system-builder

> "Read business-brief.md first. Here is the offer and content strategy to build from: [paste the outputs from Steps 2 and 3]. Do your full conversion-system-builder process: lead magnet, in-video CTA, 5-email follow-up sequence, sales path, and friction check."

## Questions to ask yourself before Step 1

These are the same questions the agents effectively asked about teddy bears and the grooming van — answering them yourself first will make every agent's output sharper:

- Is your business idea itself the product you're selling, or is it proof of something bigger (a system, a skill, a service) that's the *actual* offer?
- Is your business transactional (one-time purchase, cart-based) or recurring (appointments, subscriptions, retainers)? This single distinction changes what "offer mechanism" even means for you.
- What's the weakest link in your credibility right now — no case study, no track record, no reviews? Name it before the researcher does, so you can watch whether the later agents design around it.
