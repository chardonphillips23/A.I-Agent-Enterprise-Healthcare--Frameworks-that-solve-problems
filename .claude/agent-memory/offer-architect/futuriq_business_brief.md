FuturIQ is the creator/business this project's content is built for.

- **Audience:** Coaches, consultants, creators, service providers, and entrepreneurs who know AI matters but are overwhelmed by tools and don't know what business system to build first.
- **Core promise:** Help entrepreneurs build AI-powered business systems that create leverage, leads, and revenue.
- **Video format:** Sub-15-minute YouTube videos that feel practical, advanced, and immediately useful.
- **Recurring video concept:** Build a Claude Code agent team that turns one raw business idea into positioning, an offer, YouTube angles, a lead magnet, and a follow-up sequence — demonstrated live.
- **Offer path:** Every video should lead toward a downloadable "Revenue Agent" template, a workshop, or a paid AI-systems offer — see [[revenue-agent-team-pattern]].
- **Voice:** Direct, strategic, high-energy, premium, practical, no fluff.
- **Constraints (hard rules for all FuturIQ offer/content work):**
  - Never a generic "AI tools tutorial."
  - Never a basic prompt-engineering lesson.
  - Must show the explicit connection between the AI workflow and revenue.
  - Core message: the money is not in tools, the money is in systems.
  - Must be beginner-friendly without becoming basic/watered-down.

**Why:** This brief (`business-brief.md` at project root) is the source of truth for every offer-architecture task in this project. Read it first each time in case it changes.

**How to apply:** Treat any demoed business idea (e.g., teddy bears, or future ideas) as a proof-of-concept vehicle for FuturIQ's real product — the AI agent system/template — never as the actual thing being sold to the video audience.
